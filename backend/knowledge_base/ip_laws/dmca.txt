DMCA - Digital Millennium Copyright Act

Section 512 - Safe Harbor Provisions:
Online service providers are not liable for copyright infringement by users if they:
1. Have no knowledge of infringing activity
2. Do not receive financial benefit from infringement
3. Respond expeditiously to takedown notices

DMCA Takedown Notice Requirements:
1. Physical or electronic signature of copyright owner
2. Identification of copyrighted work
3. Identification of infringing material and its location
4. Contact information of complainant
5. Good faith statement
6. Statement of accuracy under penalty of perjury

Penalties for Copyright Infringement:
- Statutory damages: $750 to $30,000 per work
- Willful infringement: up to $150,000 per work
- Criminal penalties: up to 5 years imprisonment
- Civil penalties: actual damages plus profits

Sports Media Specific:
- Live sports broadcasts are protected under copyright
- Unauthorized streaming of sports events violates DMCA
- Sports logos, team names, player images are protected
- Official match footage cannot be redistributed without license
